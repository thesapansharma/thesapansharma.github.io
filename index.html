The VixioX difference

Why AI-native architecture changes everything

Most software agencies treat AI as a plugin. We treat it as the foundation. Here's what that means in practice.

01
Data never leaves your server
Running Qwen, Mistral or Llama locally means your sensitive business data — patients, students, financials — is processed entirely on your own infrastructure. Zero data sent to OpenAI or any third party. Zero recurring token costs. Full GDPR and data-sovereignty compliance by default.
02
Memory that actually works
Our RAG pipelines with ChromaDB give your AI products semantic memory over your own documents. Instead of a generic chatbot, you get a system that can answer "What were Q3 interventions for struggling students?" or "Which clauses in this contract carry financial risk?" — from your own data.
03
Agents that do, not just talk
We build multi-agent systems: one agent plans the task, another executes it, a third verifies the output. This means AI that can draft a report, check its own logic, and flag what it's uncertain about — not a chatbot that just sounds confident.
04
You own everything
Every line of code, every model weight, every database belongs to you. Subdomains under your brand. Source code delivered. No vendor lock-in to any AI provider. If we succeed, you own a defensible AI product — not a subscription to someone else's platform.
Questions answered

Frequently asked questions

What does "AI-native" actually mean?
It means AI is designed into the architecture before the first line of feature code is written. We plan the LLM integration, RAG pipelines, vector memory and agent workflows upfront — not bolted on later. The result is a system where intelligence is structural, not cosmetic.
How fast is the 48h MVP claim?
After a discovery call and architecture session (usually day one), we start the first sprint immediately. Within 48 hours of sprint start, you get a live, working build on your subdomain. Not a mockup — a real, deployed backend with functional AI. You see it before any further commitment.
Do I need to understand AI to work with you?
Not at all. You need to understand your business problem and your users. We translate that into the right AI architecture. Every sprint includes plain-language explanations and demos — no technical jargon required from your side.
What industries do you work with?
Our pipeline spans EdTech, healthcare, HR/recruitment, retail, legal, finance, and real estate. We've found AI adds the most value where there are large documents, complex decisions, or repetitive expert tasks. If your business has those, we can likely build something powerful for you.
How much does it cost?
Projects typically start at ₹50,000 for a focused MVP and scale based on scope. We're transparent about pricing from the first call — no hidden fees, no retainers unless you want ongoing tuning. Use the contact form below and select your budget range to get a tailored estimate.
Who owns the code and the AI models?
You do — completely. Full source code handoff, all model weights and database exports, deployed under your subdomain and brand. We believe the best long-term relationship is one where you're not dependent on us to keep the lights on. We build for ownership, not lock-in.
Can you work with my existing team or codebase?
Yes. We can embed as an AI engineering team alongside your developers, add AI capabilities to an existing product, or integrate with your current stack. Our FastAPI backends are built to connect cleanly with React, Next.js, mobile apps, or any REST consumer.
